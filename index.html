About this project

This tool visualizes daily sea surface temperature (SST) and detects Marine Heatwaves (MHWs) for Jimbaran, Nusa Dua, and Sanur. Data are aggregated to daily means; MHWs follow the 90th percentile threshold method.

Baseline derived from long-term climatology (clim, p90). Current observations are archived to a CSV and used for charting. Built with Chart.js, Leaflet, PapaParse, and Open‑Meteo Marine API.
